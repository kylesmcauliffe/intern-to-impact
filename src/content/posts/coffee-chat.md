## Why coffee chats matter

Nobody handed me a playbook. I applied to over 100 internships and learned that **one conversation** often mattered more than fifty cold applications. A coffee chat is not a formal interview — it is a short conversation to learn about someone's role, path, and advice.

## Before you message someone

1. **Research** — Know their company and role in one sentence.
2. **Be specific** — "I'd love 15 minutes to learn about your path into sales at Uber" beats "Can we chat?"
3. **Make it easy** — Offer 2–3 time windows or ask what works for them.

## What to ask

- What does your day-to-day actually look like?
- What did you wish you knew before your first internship?
- What skills made the biggest difference early in your career?
- Is there anyone else you'd recommend I speak with?

## After the chat

Send a thank-you within 24 hours. Mention one thing you learned. Keep it short.
