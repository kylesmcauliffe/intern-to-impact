## Why track everything

When you're applying from November through May, you will forget who you emailed, who ghosted, and who needs a follow-up. A simple tracker is your second brain.

## Columns to use

| Column | What to log |
|--------|-------------|
| Company | Name |
| Role | Job title / internship |
| Applied | Date |
| Status | Applied / Phone screen / Interview / Offer / Rejected |
| Contact | Anyone you messaged or met |
| Follow-up | Date to check in again |
| Notes | What went well, what to improve |

## What I learned

- The **first 50** applications taught me less than the **last 50** — I improved my resume and outreach each round.
- **Follow-ups matter** — polite, brief, not pushy.
- One offer (Uber) came after months of reps — not because the first try failed, but because I kept going.

## Start small

Apply consistently. Track honestly. Adjust weekly.
