## Connection request (short)

> Hi [Name] — I'm a [school/year] student interested in [field]. I'd love to connect and learn from your experience at [Company]. Thank you!

## Coffee chat ask

> Hi [Name], I hope you're doing well. I'm exploring [role/industry] and noticed your path into [Company]. Would you have 15 minutes for a virtual coffee chat? I'm happy to adapt to your schedule. Thank you for considering!

## After they say yes

> Thank you so much! [Day/time] works great for me. I'll send a calendar invite. Looking forward to learning from you.

## Thank-you (within 24 hours)

> Hi [Name], thank you again for taking the time today. I especially appreciated [specific thing they said]. I'll [action you promised]. Wishing you a great week!
